Need Help with Your Flight?

Contacting Delta Airlines® customer support is simple with several options, including phone, live chat, and email. Whether you're making changes to your booking or requesting a refund, assistance is available 24/7.

Call 24/7: 1-855-742-0149 (Live Person - U.S./UK/Canada/Australia)

This is the main number to speak directly with a Delta Airlines® representative.

Why Call a Live Agent?

  • Flight Disruptions: Rebooking or refunds due to delays or cancellations.
  • Complex Bookings: Group travel, special assistance, or multi-leg itineraries.
  • Refunds and Compensation: Assistance with claims and reimbursements.
  • Technical Issues: Problems with payments or website errors.
  • Peace of Mind: Clarify reservations or travel concerns.

Customer Support Options

📞 Phone Support

Call 1-855-742-0149 to reach a live Delta Airlines® agent. Follow the voice prompts and say "agent" or "representative" for quicker assistance.

💬 Live Chat

Visit the Delta Airlines® Contact Us page to access the Live Chat feature for real-time support.

📱 Mobile App

Use the Delta Airlines® app to connect with support, check flights, or manage your booking on the go.

📧 Email Support

For non-urgent issues, use the email form found on the Delta Help Center. Expect a response within 24–48 hours.

📱 Social Media

Delta Airlines® is responsive on Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram. Avoid sharing personal details in public messages.

Tips to Get Faster Help

  • Have your booking reference and flight details ready.
  • Be clear and concise when describing your issue.
  • If you’re stuck in the phone menu, try repeating “agent” to bypass prompts.

Call 1-855-742-0149 (24/7 Live Support) or use the Live Chat on the official Delta Airlines® website.
